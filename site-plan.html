Site Name

Adventure Rafting

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about white water rafting adventures, promote tour packages, and allow customers to book their trips online.

Target Audience

The site is targeted at adventurous individuals, families, and tourists who are interested in outdoor water activities and want to explore rafting destinations.

Branding

Logo, colors, and typography will reflect adventure, nature, and professionalism. Example color scheme: blue (water), green (nature), and gray (neutral).
